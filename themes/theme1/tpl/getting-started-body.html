Getting Started

  • Step 1

    Get Hardware

    In order to get started mining, you need mining  hardware. You can mine coins with CPU, GPU,  RaspberryPi, Mobile Phone or specialized  ASIC chips.

    Get Wallet Software

    In order to mine on our NewBull pool, you have  first download a wallet software from  {$currency} ({$symbol}) homepage .

  • Step 2

    Create Payment Address

    You have to create a payment address on your  wallet software in order to get your payments.

    Get Mining Software

    Next, you need a mining software that can  connect our pools. Check our  mining software list   to find a suitable one for you.

    Configure Your Miner

    Finally, you have to configure your miner's to  work against these stratum servers.
